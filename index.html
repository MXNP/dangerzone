CS: Danger Zone Competitive

Rules

  1. → Do not use cheat commands.

  2. → Choose (1) primary and (1) secondary item.

  3. → At the start of each round, you may replenish any primary/secondary items that have been used only.

  4. Guide to commands list below: Maximum you can have in (orange), maximum items of the same type per team per round in [yellow]

  • Primary

  • give weapon_bumpmine (3)
  • ent_create dronegun (2)
  • ent_create func_survival_c4_target (1) [max per team 1]
  • ent_create prop_exploding_barrel (2)
  • give item_heavyassaultsuit (1) [max per team 2]

  • Secondary

  • give weapon_shield (1)
  • give weapon_breachcharge (1, gives 3)
  • give weapon_tagrenade (3)
  • give weapon_healthshot (4) [max per team 1]
  • ent_create ent_snowball_pile (3)
  • exosuit (1)

Spawning melee

  1. → Drop knife.

  2. → Spawn item first. See list below for commands.

  3. → Use [ENT_FIRE] command, then pickup any melee item.

  • Melee commands

  • [ENT_FIRE command] give weapon_fists; ent_fire weapon_* addoutput "classname weapon_knifegg
  • [Axe] give weapon_axe
  • [Hammer] give weapon_hammer
  • [Spanner] give weapon_spanner
